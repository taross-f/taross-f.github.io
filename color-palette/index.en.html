Color palettes are collections of colors that work well together in design. Different palette types create different visual effects:

  • Monochromatic: Different shades and tints of the same color
  • Analogous: Colors that are next to each other on the color wheel
  • Complementary: Colors opposite each other on the color wheel
  • Triadic: Three colors evenly spaced on the color wheel
  • Tetradic: Four colors forming a rectangle on the color wheel
